Movie Summary

Interstellar is a science-fiction epic that follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet after Earth becomes unlivable. The movie explores time, space, love, sacrifice, and humanity’s survival beyond Earth.

Main Actors

The movie stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Dr. Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand. Each actor delivers a powerful performance that adds emotional depth to the story.
